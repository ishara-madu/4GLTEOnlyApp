Features

Powerful Tools for Network Analysis

Everything you need to monitor, analyze, and optimize your cellular connection in one beautifully designed app.

Real-time Signal Monitoring

Track RSRP, RSRQ, RSSI, and SINR signal metrics with live updates every 2 seconds. Get instant feedback on connection quality.

Speed Testing

Measure download and upload speeds with Cloudflare-powered tests. Track results over time with visual charts and statistics.

Carrier Information

View carrier name, network type, MCC/MNC codes, Cell ID, and frequency band details. Know your network inside out.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual charts for signal history, speed test results, and ping latency trends. Understand your network performance over time.

LTE/5G Switcher

Quick access to force LTE network mode via system settings. Optimize your connection for stability and performance.

Dark Mode

Beautiful neumorphic design with light, dark, and auto themes. Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition.

Data Privacy

All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud storage, no tracking. Your privacy is our priority.

Smart Reminders

Set daily speed test reminders to keep track of your network performance. Get notified when it's time to test.

Ping Testing

Measure network latency with ping tests. Track ping history to identify network issues and optimize for gaming or video calls.
