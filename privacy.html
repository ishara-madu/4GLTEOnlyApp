Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Ishara Madhusanka ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Force LTE Only mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the app. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

While using our app, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include:

  • Email address (if you contact us for support)
  • Name and profile information (if you choose to provide it)

Usage Data

We may also collect information that your device sends whenever you use our app ("Usage Data"). This may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and configuration
  • Signal strength measurements, Cell Info, and precise LTE Band data (stored locally only, never shared)
  • Speed test results (stored locally only)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Force LTE Only stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Signal strength history (RSRP, RSRQ, RSSI, SINR)
  • Speed test results
  • Ping test results
  • Data usage statistics
  • App settings and preferences

This local data is protected by your device's security settings and is not accessible by us or any third parties.

Cloud Storage

We do not upload, transfer, or store any of your personal data or usage data on external servers. All data collected through the app remains on your device.

Third-Party Services and Advertising

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to securely manage premium subscriptions and purchase history. RevenueCat may collect necessary information to process and maintain your subscription status. For more information, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Google AdMob

We may use third-party advertising services, specifically Google AdMob, to display advertisements within the app. These services may collect certain information about you, including device identifiers and advertising ID.

For information about how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare Speed Test

Our speed test feature uses Cloudflare's servers. For information about Cloudflare's privacy practices, please visit Cloudflare Privacy.

Permissions We Request

Our app requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Phone State - Required to read signal strength and network information
  • Location - Required by Android to fetch precise Cell Info and LTE Band data. This data remains strictly on your device and is never shared.
  • Internet - Required for speed test functionality
  • Notifications - Used for speed test reminders (Android 13+)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights:

  • Access - Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification - Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data portability - Request transfer of your data to another organization

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
